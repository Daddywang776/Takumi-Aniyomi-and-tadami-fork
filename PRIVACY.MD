# Takumi Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-15

Takumi is designed to help you manage and synchronize your anime, manga, and novel library data. This Privacy Policy explains what information Takumi may process and how it is handled.

## Information Takumi Processes

Takumi may process the following data locally on your device:

- Library metadata and reading data
- Backup and restore data created by Takumi
- Takumi preferences and settings
- A locally generated device identifier used for sync conflict resolution

If you enable Google Drive sync, Takumi also processes:

- Google OAuth access and refresh tokens
- Google Drive file metadata needed for synchronization
- Backup data uploaded to or downloaded from your Google Drive app data storage

## How the Data Is Used

Takumi uses this data only to:

- Display and manage your anime, manga, and novel library
- Create and restore local backups
- Synchronize your data between devices when you enable Google Drive sync
- Detect which device last performed a sync

## Storage

- Most Takumi data is stored locally on your device.
- If you enable Google Drive sync, synchronization data is stored in Google Drive app data storage.
- OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device so Takumi can maintain your Google Drive session.

## Sharing

Takumi does not sell your personal data.

Takumi only sends data to Google when you explicitly use Google Drive sign-in or synchronization features. In that case, Google may process the data according to its own terms and privacy policy.

## Permissions and Access

When you sign in with Google Drive, you grant Takumi access only to the scopes required for synchronization:

- `drive.file`
- `drive.appdata`

## Your Choices

You can:

- Use Takumi without Google Drive sync
- Disable cloud sync at any time
- Sign out from Google Drive
- Remove synchronization data from Google Drive through Takumi, if available

## Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall Takumi.

If Google Drive sync is enabled, synchronization data remains in your Google Drive until you delete it or remove Takumi's access.

OAuth tokens are removed when you sign out or clear Takumi's data.

## Children

Takumi is not intended for use by children without appropriate supervision.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published in this repository.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the project's GitHub issues page or the maintainer contact listed in the repository.
